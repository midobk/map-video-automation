# Fixture map asset

## File

- `world.svg`

## Source

Simplified, abstract continental shapes derived from public-domain
Natural Earth 1:110m Cultural Vectors (continents and major landmasses),
heavily stylized and redrawn for use as a neutral test fixture.

- Natural Earth URL: https://www.naturalearthdata.com/
- License: public domain
- Version used: 5.1.2
- Date accessed: 2026-07-15

## Purpose

Deterministic, offline map-video fixture for the Map Video Automation
renderer. Contains no disputed boundaries, labels, current events, or
proprietary data.

## Redistribution

This SVG is released as a public-domain test fixture and may be committed
to the repository.
